## Purpose

These soil extracts must be run on SDS-PAGE in order to be analyzed by mass spec. Specifically because this disrupts the secondary structure of misfolded prion protein.

## Design
- Samples were added to the gel in such a way that the total protein in the well equals 285 ng.
- Gels were stored in 50% MetOH/10% acetic acid until ready for silver staining.
