## Purpose

Part of this project is modelling classical enzyme kinetics onto the RT-QuIC reaction. In order to do that we must test the substrate and seed at multiple dilutions. This experiment focuses on a dilution series of the positive control sample.

## Design
- Testing from $10^{-3}\rightarrow10^{-6}$ in 24 evenly log-spaced increments.
- 42$\textdegree$C, 72h, 15min reads
- Replicated on 3 different plates
