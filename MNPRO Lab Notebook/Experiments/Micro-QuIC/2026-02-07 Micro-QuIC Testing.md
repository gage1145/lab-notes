## Purpose

We are trying to determine if we can get mixing and amplification with the Micro-QuIC flow cell.

## Design
1. Frequency: 6500 Hz
	1. 1 min on, 1 min off
2. Temp: 50$\textdegree$C
3. Reaction volume: 20$\textmu$L
## Results

I think 50C is too high. I noticed condensation within the view port. This likely would have changed the reaction mix. Additionally, liquid was observed migrating through the snake which altered the side channel bubble morphologies. This would have altered the mixing efficiency.

It is likely that some seeded material was observed with fluorescent microscopy, but I am non-committal on that stance.
