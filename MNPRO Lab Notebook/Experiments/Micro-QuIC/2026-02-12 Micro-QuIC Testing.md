## Purpose

Continuing testing. We found that 50$\textdegree$C was too high, so we are lowering to 37. Additionally, because there was empty volume in the flow cell, liquid was allowed to migrate and change the morphology of the side channel bubbles. This reduced the mixing efficiency; the whole flow cell volume will be used this time.

## Design
1. Frequency: 6500 Hz
2. 1 min on, 1 min off
3. Temp: 37$\textdegree$C
4. Reaction volume: full (not sure the total volume)
5. Experiment started at 10:05am
