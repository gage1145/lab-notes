## Equipment
- Tube rotator
- Refrigerated tabletop centrifuge
## Consumables
- 500mg soil sample
- MSB buffer
	- 0.6mM myristyl sulfobetaine
	- 75.4mM dibasic sodium phosphate
	- 24.6mM monobasic sodium phosphate
	- pH 7.7
- Aminoethyl benzenesulfonyl fluoride hydrochloride (AEBSF)
- diH$_2$O
- NaPTA solution
	- 4% (w:v) sodium phosphotungstate
	- 150mM magnesium chloride

## Protocol
1. Add 200mg of dried soil to a 1.5mL centrifuge tube.
2. Add 1000$\textmu{}$L MSB buffer to the soil.
3. Rotate at room temperature for 60 min.
4. Spin at 8000xg, 10 min, RT.
5. Transfer 750$\textmu$L of the supernatant to a new 1.5mL tube.
6. Add an additional 250$\textmu$L MSB buffer to the pellet.
7. Rotate for another 60 min.
8. Spin at 8000xg, 10 min, RT.
9. Transfer 250$\textmu$L to the original supernatant.
10. Add 20$\textmu$L AEBSF to the consolidated supernatant.
11. Vortex.
12. Spin at 8000xg, 10 min, RT.
13. Transfer ~950$\textmu$L of the supernatant while avoiding the pellet to a new 1.5mL tube.
14. Add 90$\textmu$L of NaPTA solution to the supernatant.
15. Incubate without mixing for 1 hr, RT.
16. Spin at 21,000xg, 30 min, 4$\textdegree$C.
17. Carefully discard the supernatant.
18. Add 200$\textmu$L diH$_2$O to the pellet without disturbing the pellet.
19. Spin at 21,000xg, 30 min, 4$\textdegree$C.
20. Carefully discard the supernatant.
21. Freeze pellets at -80$\textdegree$C.
